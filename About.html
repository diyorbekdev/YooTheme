About us

At YOOtheme we're passionate about developing everything you need to create beautiful websites with WordPress and Joomla. As the market leader for Joomla themes and extensions with 10 years of experience we have one of the largest communities. We are also known as the creators of the popular Open Source projects UIkit and Pagekit. Our small, diverse team from Hamburg, Germany, combines love for design with modern technology to make the web look good.

2007

In 2007 Sascha and Steffan founded
YOOtheme. What began as a little
project in Sascha's basement soon became a
thriving success.

150K

Over 150,000 customers trust our
products to build and design their websites.
It is our goal to create the most cutting-edge
web software for them.

500K

We share a passion for Open Source.
Over half a million websites were built
with our most popular project, the front-end
mt-5 framework UIkit.
